Common Questions

Learn more about LG Subscribe services, subscription terms, and how our consultation process works.

What is LG Subscribe? +

LG Subscribe is an innovative rental program that lets you use premium LG appliances without paying the full price upfront. Simply pay affordable monthly installments, and after the contract period (typically 5-7 years), the appliance becomes yours permanently.

Is there any deposit required? +

No deposit is required! That's the beauty of LG Subscribe. You can start enjoying your LG appliances immediately with no initial payment payment. Just pay the first month's rental fee.

What does the maintenance service include? +

The subscription includes comprehensive maintenance for 5-7 years: regular home visits for cleaning and inspection, filter/consumable replacements, interior and exterior cleaning, and product performance checks. All at no extra cost.

Can I subscribe to multiple products? +

Absolutely! Many customers subscribe to multiple products like water purifier, air purifier, and washing machine together. We can design a bundled package for you with better rates.

How do I pay the monthly rental? +

Monthly payments are automatically deducted from your debit or credit card via auto-debit. It's convenient and hassle-free. We will assist you in setting this up during the application process.

Can I terminate the contract early? +

Early termination is possible but subject to termination fees calculated based on your usage duration and remaining contract period. We recommend choosing the right contract length from the start to avoid this.

Is Our consultation service free? +

Yes! Our consultation service is completely free of charge. As an LG Authorised Consultant, We provide product recommendations, home assessments, and subscription planning at no cost to you.

What happens after the contract ends? +

Once your contract period is complete, the appliance becomes your permanent property with no additional payment required. You can continue using it or choose to upgrade to a newer model with a new subscription.
